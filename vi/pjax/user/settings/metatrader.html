Financial Account

In order to create an account for MetaTrader, you need to authenticate your Binary account.

To authenticate your account, kindly email the following to support@binary.com

  • A scanned copy of your passport, driving licence (provisional or full) or identity card, showing your name and date of birth.
  • A scanned copy of a utility bill or bank statement (no more than 3 months old).

To deposit or withdraw from your MetaTrader Account, switch to your Binary.com Real Account.
